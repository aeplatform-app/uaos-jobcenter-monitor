# Demo Video Script 001

## Title
Sing Into UAOS, Get a Full Song

## Hook
What if you could sing one melody and instantly turn it into a full produced track?

## Scene 1
User sings melody into microphone.

## Scene 2
UAOS detects pitch, rhythm, tempo, and key.

## Scene 3
UAOS generates:
- MIDI melody
- chords
- drums
- bass
- arrangement

## Scene 4
Export options:
- MIDI
- WAV
- stems
- DAW project

## CTA
Universal Arranger OS  turn ideas into music.
