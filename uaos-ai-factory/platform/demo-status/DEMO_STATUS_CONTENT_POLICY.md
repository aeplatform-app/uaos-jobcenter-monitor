# Demo Status Content Policy

Content must be honest, concise, and clearly marked as prototype/demo/staging.

Allowed:
- Current local prototype status.
- Planning documents.
- AI Factory local planning progress.
- Metadata/QA/provenance planning.
- Safe preview/staging intentions.

Blocked:
- Public launch claim.
- Production readiness claim.
- Payment activation readiness claim.
- Real writer/export completion claim.
- Commercial premium library claim.
