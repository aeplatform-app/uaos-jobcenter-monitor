# Linear Labels Statuses Plan

## Statuses

- Backlog: not ready for AI work.
- Ready: approved for scoped work.
- In Progress: actively owned.
- Review: waiting for human or senior engineer review.
- QA: validation pending.
- Blocked: cannot continue without user or external-state change.
- Waiting External Account: blocked until GitHub/Linear/Vercel/account setup exists.
- Done Local: completed locally but not externally synced.
- Done: completed and validated in the approved system.
- Deferred: intentionally parked.

## Labels

- `ai-factory`: AI Factory coordination work.
- `cost-guard`: credit or compute protection.
- `safety-gate`: safety-sensitive work.
- `no-deploy`: must not deploy.
- `no-payment`: must not activate payment.
- `no-real-writer`: must not enable real keyboard writer/export.
- `prototype`, `demo`, `staging`: honest readiness labels.
- `frontend`, `backend`, `music-engine`, `midi`, `library-metadata`: work areas.
- `vercel`, `github-transfer`, `linear`: platform coordination.
- `qa`, `bug`, `blocker`: validation and issue state.
- `risk-low`, `risk-medium`, `risk-high`: execution risk.
