# QA Failure Escalation Policy

Escalate from Copilot/local scripts to Code X when:
- A build/test failure remains after one attempt.
- The failure requires architecture judgment.
- The failure touches security, safety, payment, export/writer, or proprietary format handling.
- The task becomes high-risk or ambiguous.

Do not loop. Do not hide failing output. Do not mark failed QA as complete.
