# Owner Handoff Policy

Status: local plan only

The owner handoff pack is for private local review only. It must not be treated as a public release, production readiness announcement, deployment checklist, payment launch, or writer/export launch.

The handoff may summarize local commits, risks, transfer status, and next safe decision points. It must not change remotes, publish commits, deploy, enable payments, enable external automation, or require App.jsx modification.
