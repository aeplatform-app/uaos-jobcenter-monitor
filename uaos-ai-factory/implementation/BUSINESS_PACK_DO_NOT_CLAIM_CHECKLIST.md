# Business Pack Do Not Claim Checklist

LOCAL ONLY - OWNER REVIEW ONLY - NO SENDING

## Do Not Claim

- No production release.
- No public release.
- No public URL availability.
- No live customer use.
- No live payment or payment readiness.
- No real keyboard export.
- No `.SET` or `.STY` export support.
- No keyboard transfer readiness.
- No proprietary library/sample ownership.
- No Kontakt or Native Instruments content rights.
- No external automation readiness.

## Do Not Attach

- App source code.
- Frontend source code.
- `App.jsx`.
- Deploy instructions.
- Vercel output.
- Payment setup files.
- Production release files.
- Real keyboard-native files.
- `.STY`, `.SET`, `.PRS`, `.STL`, `.PAT`, `.MSP`, or `.KST`.
- Proprietary audio, samples, Kontakt, Native Instruments, or sample libraries.
- Legacy `.STY` files or copied legacy contents.

## Attach Only After Owner Review

- Local prototype overview.
- Safe wording selected for the recipient.
- Local/static screenshots with clear labels.
- Funding or workstation/laptop note if needed.
- Safe checklist or handoff page.

## Final Owner Gate

The owner must review every file name, caption, message paragraph, and attachment before sending anything manually. This checklist does not approve sending or automation.
