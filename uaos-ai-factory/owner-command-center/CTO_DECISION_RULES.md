# CTO Decision Rules

- Prefer local-only work unless explicit deployment approval exists.
- Commit after each successful bounded agent block.
- Stop on safety failure.
- Do not claim production readiness.
- Do not enable payment.
- Do not create keyboard-native output or transfer files.
- Do not scan excluded folders unless required for existence/size checks.
- Use Jobcenter-safe wording for finance and evidence documents.
- Preserve German umlauts.
