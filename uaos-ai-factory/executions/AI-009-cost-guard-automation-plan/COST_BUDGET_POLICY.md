# Cost Budget Policy

Status: local plan only

- Monthly budget mode: strict.
- No autonomous purchase of credits.
- No automatic upgrade.
- No background 24-hour tasks.
- No repeated builds.
- No repeated retries.
- Maximum builds per task: 1 unless approved.
- Maximum retries per task: 1 unless approved.
- User approval is required before paid expansion.
- Stop immediately on rate limit, credit warning, or unclear cost risk.
