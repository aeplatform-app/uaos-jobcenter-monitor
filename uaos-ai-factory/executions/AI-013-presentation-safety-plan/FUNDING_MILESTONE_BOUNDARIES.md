# Funding Milestone Boundaries

LOCAL ONLY - NOT PUBLIC RELEASE

Status: local plan only

Funding discussions may describe what support would make possible, but must not promise guaranteed outcomes.

## Safe Milestone Examples

- Continue prototype development.
- Improve local demo materials.
- Prepare transfer and staging readiness plans.
- Improve QA and safety checks.
- Document library metadata safely.

## Required Boundaries

- No investment guarantee.
- No revenue guarantee.
- No public launch guarantee.
- No production deployment guarantee.
- No payment/live customer guarantee.
