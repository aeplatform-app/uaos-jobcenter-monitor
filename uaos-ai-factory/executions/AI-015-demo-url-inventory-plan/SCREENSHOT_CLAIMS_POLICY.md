# Screenshot Claims Policy

LOCAL ONLY - NOT PUBLIC RELEASE

Status: local plan only

- Screenshots may show local prototype/static presentation progress.
- Screenshots may support Jobcenter and supporter explanations.
- Screenshots must be labeled LOCAL ONLY or STATIC LOCAL DEMO.
- Screenshots must not imply public launch.
- Screenshots must not imply production deployment.
- Screenshots must not imply payment/live customer activity.
- Screenshots must not imply writer/export readiness.
- Screenshots must not imply proprietary sample/library ownership.
- Screenshots must not imply external automation readiness.
