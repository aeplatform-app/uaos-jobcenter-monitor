# Email DNS Protection

`aeplatform.app` has Pro Email / mailboxes planned or existing.

Protect:
- MX records.
- SPF records.
- DKIM records.
- DMARC records.

Do not move `aeplatform.app` nameservers without copying email DNS records first.

Recommended public contact: `admin@aeplatform.app` or `contact@aeplatform.app`.
